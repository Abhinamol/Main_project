FAQ

1. What types of laptop/desktop issues do you repair?

We provide comprehensive repair services for a wide range of laptop and desktop issues, including hardware and software problems. This includes but is not limited to screen replacement, keyboard repair, data recovery, virus removal, and more.

2. How long does a typical laptop/desktop repair take?

The repair time can vary depending on the complexity of the issue. Minor repairs may be completed within a few hours, while more complex problems could take a day or longer. Our technicians will provide you with an estimated repair time after assessing your device.

3. Do you offer on-site repair services?

Yes, we offer on-site repair services for your convenience. Our technicians can come to your location to diagnose and repair laptop/desktop issues. Please contact us to schedule an appointment.

4. Are my data and files safe during the repair process?

We take data privacy seriously. Before performing any repairs, we advise you to back up your important data. While we make every effort to safeguard your files, there is always a small risk involved. It's always best to have a backup as a precaution.

5. What is your pricing for laptop/desktop repairs?

Our repair pricing varies depending on the specific issue and the required replacement parts. We offer competitive and transparent pricing. Our technicians will provide you with a quote after diagnosing the problem, and we do not charge any hidden fees.

6. Do you provide a warranty for your repair services?

Yes, we stand behind the quality of our repair work. We offer a warranty on parts and labor for a specified period, ensuring that you have peace of mind knowing that your device is covered in case of any issues related to the repair.
